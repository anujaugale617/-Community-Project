# Community project
Food adulteration is one of the major issues affecting public health in India. Many food items 
such as milk, spices, oils, and sweets are adulterated with harmful substances, which can cause 
severe health problems. Our project focuses on creating awareness among community members 
about the harmful effects of food adulteration and providing knowledge through digital means.
To achieve this, we designed and developed a simple website that contains information about 
food adulteration, its harmful effects, and common methods to detect adulterated food at home. 
The website also includes preventive tips and awareness material that can be easily accessed by 
anyone.
The project not only helped us improve our technical skills in web development but also allowed 
us to contribute socially by spreading awareness digitally. We believe that this initiative can 
reach a wider audience and help people adopt safe food practices.
